## 2. What Caregiver App Is

Caregiver App is a shared coordination tool for families and small support circles. It helps a group of people jointly organise tasks, appointments, projects, and a shared daily log. It is a general-purpose collaboration and organisation tool — it is not a medical device, medical record system, or healthcare service, and it is not intended for storing sensitive health or medical information (see Section 7).

## 3. What Data We Collect

**Account data:** email address, display name, language preference, and — if you enable push notifications — a device push-notification token used solely to deliver reminders to your device.

**Circle membership:** which care circle(s) you belong to and your role within them.

**Content you and your circle create:** tasks, appointments, projects, lists and checklists, daily log entries, notes, planned absences ("vacations"), and any other text you or other members of your care circle enter into the app. This content is visible to other members of your care circle according to the visibility settings you choose.

**Information about third parties:** if you add an external contact (for example, a doctor or carer who does not use the app), we store the name, email address, and any notes you enter about them. You are responsible for having a lawful basis to share another person's information this way.

**Operational data:** a log of push notifications sent to your account (what was sent and when) and read/unread status of in-app notifications, used to ensure reminders are delivered reliably and to power the notification centre.

**Consent record:** the timestamp at which you accepted these Terms of Service and this Privacy Policy during registration.

We do not collect location data, advertising identifiers, or browsing data outside the app.

## 4. Why We Process Your Data (Legal Basis)

- **Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR):** you actively agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service at registration, via a checkbox, before your account is created.
- **Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR):** processing your account and content data is necessary to provide the coordination features you and your circle use the app for.

## 5. Where Your Data Is Stored

All account and content data is stored in a Supabase-hosted database located in the **EU (Ireland)**.

## 7. Please Do Not Enter Sensitive Health Information

Caregiver App is designed as a general coordination tool, not a health-data system. Please do not enter medical diagnoses, medication details, or other sensitive health information about yourself or anyone else into the app. If you are coordinating care for another person, you confirm that you are authorised to share the information about that person which you choose to enter into the app, and that such information does not include sensitive health data.

## 8. How Long We Keep Your Data

We keep your account and content data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion of your account (see Section 9), we delete your personal data and content within a reasonable time, except where retention is required by law.

## 9. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict or object** to certain processing
- **Data portability** (receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format)
- **Withdraw your consent** at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal

At this stage, these requests are handled manually. To exercise any of these rights, email **ofer.eisenberg@gmail.com**. We will respond within a reasonable time and no later than one month, as required by the GDPR.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority.

## 10. Minimum Age

Caregiver App is intended for users aged **16 and over**, in line with Art. 8 GDPR and German law.

## 11. Security

Access to your data is restricted using database-level access control (Row Level Security), so that circle members can only see data belonging to their own care circle. Data is encrypted in transit.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent change. Material changes will be communicated to registered users where feasible.
